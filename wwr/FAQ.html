Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for white water rafting?

Wear quick-drying, non-cotton clothing such as a swimsuit, synthetic shirt, and shorts. Bring a pair of water shoes or secure sandals. Avoid flip-flops and open-toed shoes. Depending on the weather, you might also need a wetsuit, which we can provide. Don't forget sunscreen, sunglasses with a strap, and a hat.

Do I need previous rafting experience?

No previous rafting experience is necessary. Our trips are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced rafters. Our professional guides will provide a thorough safety briefing and paddling instructions before you hit the water.

Is white water rafting safe for children?

Safety is our top priority. We offer family-friendly rafting trips suitable for children as young as 6 years old. The minimum age may vary depending on the river's difficulty level and current water conditions. Please check the specific trip details for age requirements.

What if I can't swim?

Non-swimmers are welcome! We provide all participants with a Coast Guard-approved life jacket, which must be worn at all times during the trip. Our guides are trained in water rescue and safety techniques to ensure everyone's safety, regardless of their swimming ability.

What should I bring on the rafting trip?

Bring a change of clothes, a towel, and a water bottle. If you wear glasses or sunglasses, use a strap to secure them. You might also want to bring a waterproof camera or a GoPro to capture your adventure. We provide all necessary rafting equipment, including life jackets, helmets, and paddles.

How do I make a reservation and what is your cancellation policy?

Answer: You can make a reservation online through our website or by calling our customer service team. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season. Our cancellation policy allows for a full refund if you cancel at least 7 days before your trip. Cancellations made within 7 days of the trip will be charged a 50% fee. No-shows or cancellations within 24 hours of the trip will not be refunded.
